Hello, I am Zak and welcome to my Blog!

I often find myself ranting about the seemingly mainstream misuse and under-appreciation for the context of data, statistics, or other trends reported in the media - whether it be news broadcasts, statements by Members of Parliament, or the latest social media bandwagon spreading misinfortmation.

Because of this, I spend a lot of time researching many claims seen in the media. Fairly regularly, I think to myself - “heh, I just done enough research to write MY OWN article” - and so I eventually decided to just that.

So yeah, basically, I just post my own opinions or essays on various topics, backed by proper research and data.

Hope you enjoy! If you do, feel free to email me or connect on twitter. I am always happy to receive feedback or suggestions for a topic.

A little bit about me…

I am a trained software programmer, specialising in Web Technologies, Data Warehousing and Analysis, with a little bit of competency in Machine Learning (though, “Artificial Intelligence” sounds WAY cooler). I am also no stranger to research and distinguishing real facts from false news, I have been author or co-author to several peer-reviewed research publications.

Here are some cool things that might be noteworthy about me:

  • I have been programming since I got my first computer at 14 years of age
  • Completed a Bachelor of Information Technology, with 1st Class Honours
  • Completed the formative period of a PhD program up to candiditure, which is now on hold indefinitely
  • Received multiple merit-based scholarships for Overseas travel to academic conferences
  • Nominated for Vice-Chairman and Seceretary of the IEEE LTSC xAPI Technical Advisory Group (TAG)
  • Participated in peer-review panel and committee member for several conferences and advisory groups, some of which include:
    • IEEE LTSC TAG Subcommittee Member for the xAPI Specification Version 1.0.3
    • ASCILITE ‘16 and LAK Conferences ('16, '17, and ‘18)
  • Cumulative 10+ years of Data Science and Software Development experience
