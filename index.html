Statistics about Men will always be wrong until prejudice is removed from data collection

20 Nov 2020, 12:17PM | 1.5k words | 9 minutes
In recognition of International Men's Day 2020, I decided that I would spend a day reviewing the problems facing men in contemporary Australian society. Instead of finding data of comparable quality to other demographics, I was instead disheartened to find that our own publicly funded organisations are not concerned about men, their issues, or their contributions within our society.
